My Three Companies

Topic #1: The Green Thumb

The Green Thumb is a company that caters to avid plant lovers and gardening enthusiasts. They would sell various types of houseplants, as well as unique planters and pots. They would also offer more hands-on options such as grow-your-own plant kits that would include seed packets, potting soil, and planter, and terrariums that would arrive disassembled with detailed care instructions. The Green Thumb would offer maintenance materials for experienced gardeners such as watering cans and garden tool sets, as well.

Topic #2: Paper People

Paper People is a company that sells unique stationery products from around the world. They would offer a wide variety of planners, calendars, and cute sticker sheets. Their products would also include many types of pens, washi tapes, and memo pads. Paper People would offer stationery bundles as well called “deco kits” which would include stickers, memo pads, and washi tapes that follow a variety of themes the customers could choose from. This could also function as a monthly or annual subscription package.

Topic #3: Fresh Face

Fresh Face is a company that offers organic and ethically-sourced skincare and makeup products. They would offer all-natural items such as face masks, scrubs, serums, and lotions. Although of a smaller selection, they would also sell makeup products like mascaras, lipsticks, lip glosses, blushes, foundation, and eye shadow palettes. They would offer a variety of different fragrances, styles, and ingredients for each product to cater to each customer, and many different tones and swatches for the available makeup products.
